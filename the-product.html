-- HOW IT WORKS --

Our first product called Recharger allows dual charging capabilities:
1. Traditional standard wall charge
2. Translated kinetic motion to electrical energy that requires low time investment
It’s portable lightweight design also includes switchable connection charging heads (micro-usb, iOS devices, wall charge) stored in the device for easy access as well as attachable accessories.

-- HOW do i use it --

Battery low? Simply twist the device until the status bar lights green, attach the correct head for your device and connect to charge your mobile.
Don’t need it right away? Charge can be stored for later uses.
