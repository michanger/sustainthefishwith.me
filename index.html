-- ABOUT US --

The World's fish stock is declining. Seafood consumption is on the rise- and for good reasons, Fish is high in healthy fats like omega 3 and low in cholesterol. The result is one of the healthiest protein sources known for man. Another touted advantage of fish, namely the low energy required to bring a pound of meat to the dinner table hides a problem. Fish is the last major animal that we hunt in the wild. Chickens have been domesticated. Cattle have been domesticated. Together with humans they are amung the most populous animal species on the planet. All sorts of fish species, on the other hand, are not doing as well. As humanity's appetite for fish increase, we must develop alternative ways to produce fish- currently the concept of sustainable fish relies on low production, preserving fish stocks but keeping prices high. We are interested in a future where fish are plentiful, cheap, and a viable healthy alternative in our everyday diet.
